Why we exist

Land in Tanzania shouldn't require insider knowledge.

Too many buyers — especially the diaspora — lose money to double-sold plots, missing surveys, and paperwork that goes nowhere. We built Wakanda Land to make every step verifiable.

Surveyed & beaconed

Every plot is professionally surveyed with beacons placed and GPS coordinates recorded before it's listed.

Title-ready paperwork

We manage the documentation trail end-to-end, from sale agreement to certificate of title.

Pay from anywhere

Flexible installments via M-Pesa or bank transfer — built for buyers at home and abroad.

The process

From first visit to title deed, in four clear steps.

STEP 01

Choose a parcel

Browse live inventory online or book a free site visit. Every listing shows real coordinates, size, and status.

STEP 02

Verify everything

We walk you through the survey plan, ownership history, and local authority records — before you pay a shilling.

STEP 03

Reserve & pay

Secure your plot with a deposit via M-Pesa, then pay flexibly in installments that fit your plan.

STEP 04

Receive your title

We process the transfer and hand you your documents. Your land, your name, on record.
